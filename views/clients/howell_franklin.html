Case: Legal Advice

Assgined Attorney: Holden M.

Howell

Ingredients
1 ½ lbs ground bison
1 bag tater tots
1 can condensed cream of mushroom soup
½ bag green beans, defrosted
1 ½ cups shredded cheddar cheese
A bunch of bacon, pre-cooked
Half a thing of those crispy onion things
½ teaspoon salt
½ teaspoon pepper
Worcester, a splash or two.
 Steps
1. Preheat oven to 350° F.
2. Brown bison with a splash or two of worcester, drain.
3. Mix browned bison, salt, pepper, soup and green beans. Pour in casserole.
4. Place tater tots on top of beef mixture. Then top off with cheddar cheese, bacon (crumbled) and those crispy onion things.
5. Cover with foil and bake 45 minutes. Remove foil and bake 15 minutes.
6. Serve immediately.
